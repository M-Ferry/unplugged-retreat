Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

To reach the unplugged retreat, grab an old-fashioned paper map. Head north until you find a tree that looks like it's waving at you and take a left. Continue straight past the squirrel marathon meadow. At the fork, choose right if cows are on the left and vice versa. Cross the creaky bridge and follow the mushroom trail in the forest. When you spot the ancient oak with a carved face, take the hidden path behind it. Follow the sound of the babbling brook until you reach a cabin with a “Welcome to the Unplugged Life” sign. You've arrived—time to switch off and relax!

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

The perfect team for an Unplugged retreat is one that can survive without Wi-Fi for more than five minutes. Ideally, they should be able to remember what a conversation without emojis looks like and can navigate using a compass instead of Google Maps. Extra points if they can light a campfire without asking Alexa for help and tell a ghost story that doesn't start with "Once upon a meme..."!

What size teams can you accommodate?

We can accommodate teams of all sizes, from small groups who can fit around a single campfire to larger squads who might need to form a human chain to find their way back from a nature walk. Whether you have a handful of folks who can all squeeze into one hammock or a crowd big enough to play an epic game of tug-of-war with a tree, we got you covered. Just remember, the more people you bring, the more marshmallows you will need for the s'mores!

Is there really no wifi available?

No Wi-Fi? Absolutely! We've even trained the squirrels to confiscate any hidden hotspots. Your emails will have to wait, but on the bright side, you'll have plenty of time to ponder life's big questions, like why you never learned to start a campfire or how to entertain yourself without a screen. Trust us, the strongest signal you'll find here is the one from the nearest owl hooting goodnight!
