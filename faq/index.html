Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

To reach the unplugged retreat, first toss your GPS out the window—nature will guide you, or at least the squirrels will. Follow the dirt road until you question your life choices, then keep going. When you see a sign that says "No Wi-Fi, No Worries," you're halfway there. Finally, take a deep breath, because your phone signal has officially ghosted you. You've arrived—time to switch off and relax!

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

The best team for an unplugged retreat is one that panics when their phones hit 1%, but claims they could totally survive in the wild. They should consist of at least one person who thinks they can start a fire with sheer willpower and another who believes snacks are a survival necessity. Bonus points if no one has ever pitched a tent, but everyone's watched at least three seasons of "Survivor."

What size teams can you accommodate?

The unplugged retreat can accommodate teams of any size, from a small group of introverts who want to avoid each other in the woods to a large crowd that will realize they’re terrible at group bonding without Wi-Fi. Whether you’re a duo looking to find inner peace or a horde ready to discover who cracks first without TikTok, we’ve got you covered!

Is there really no wifi available?

Yep, no Wi-Fi—just you, nature, and the terrifying realization that you'll have to talk to each other.
